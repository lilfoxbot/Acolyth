▼ inspiration

▼ ideas
- small enclosed voxel environments
- each environment is filled with white/black tar that spreads
- "structures" on map that activate when touched by tar
- win condition: white tar reaches black crystal
- fail condition: black tar reaches white crystal
- swap between first-person and top-down views
- first-person player can shoot and place turrets to fight tar
- top-down player can manipulate structures
- blastoff transition between levels (tempest)

▼ structures
- crystal: win condition
- gate: allows swap between first-person and top-down
- turret: shoots nearby tar
